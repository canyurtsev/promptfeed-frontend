help Quick Help / FAQ

How do I submit a new prompt? expand_more

Click the "Submit Prompt" button on the main page. Fill in the title, description, and content, then publish.

How does the ranking system work? expand_more

Posts are ranked using a formula that considers upvotes, downvotes, age, and boost status. Fresh, high-quality content rises faster.

What is the "Boost" feature? expand_more

Boost temporarily increases your prompt's visibility in the feed. It's a paid feature available to PRO users.

How do I report a violation? expand_more

Use the "..." menu on any post or comment to find the Report option. Our moderation team reviews all reports.
